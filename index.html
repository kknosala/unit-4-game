Crystal Collector Game

Rules

You will be given a target number at the beginning of the game.

The crystal below will have a random number assigned to them. When you click on them, their number will be added to your total. The only way to see how much a crystal is worth is to check how much is added to your total.

You must try to make your total match the target number. If you do, you win! If not, you lose.

Either way, the game restarts with a new target number and new values for the crystals.

Good Luck!
